# Pomona Water Irrigation Risk v0.1

Pomona Water Irrigation Risk v0.1 is a local scaffold for training a small specialist reasoner that classifies irrigation and water risks from structured farm sensor packets.

## Intended Use

- Detect missing or suspect irrigation telemetry.
- Flag low/high water level and dry/wet substrate patterns.
- Identify pump-flow and valve-state conflicts.
- Suggest safe next checks before any irrigation action.

## Not Intended For

- Autonomous pump or valve control.
- Definitive crop stress diagnosis.
- Chemical or fertigation dosage recommendations.

## Status

Local scaffold only. Not published to Hugging Face.
